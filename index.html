Welcome to the Water Health Index

The Water Health Index helps you quickly check local water quality using a simple test strip, your phone camera, and your location.

After recent trends in outdoor activities and exploration, our tool lets everyone assess the safety and suitability of water in real-time. Simply measure pH, note temperature, select water clarity (turbidity), then set your location and see your result.
