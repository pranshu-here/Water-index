Welcome to Water Health Index

"Clean water is a right, not a privilege."

Harness the power of technology and community to measure, monitor, and share water quality globally. With simple tools and your location, join the movement to protect water for all.

How to get started:

  • Navigate to the Measure page to enter pH, temperature, and turbidity data.
  • Use your device camera and location to help capture data quickly and accurately.
  • Save your readings and contribute to our global water health map.
  • Explore more resources, charts and farming assistance via the navigation above.
